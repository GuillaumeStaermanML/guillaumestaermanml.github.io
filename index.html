About me

I am a post doctoral researcher in the Parietal team at INRIA Paris-Saclay, since may 2022, working with Thomas Moreau and Alexandre Gramfort. I work on point processes and their application to model stimuli induced patterns in M/EEG signals.

Prior to this position, I did my PhD at Télécom Paris (Institut Polytechnique de Paris) in the Team Signal, Statistique et Apprentissage (S2A). My advisors were Pavlo Mozharovskyi, Stéphan Clémençon and Florence d'Alché-Buc. My PhD focuses on anomaly detection with a special focus on time-series/functional data as well as robust estimation applied to a wide variety of ML problems such as probability metrics estimation.
