About me

I am a PhD student at Télécom Paris (Institut Polytechnique de Paris) in the Team Signal, Statistique et Apprentissage (S2A) since January 2019. My advisors are Pavlo Mozharovskyi, Stéphan Clémençon and Florence d'Alché-Buc.

Research Interests

My research interest lies at the boundary between machine learning, statistics and signal processing. My work focuses on anomaly detection and robust estimation with a special focus on time-series/functional data. Furthermore, i have a particular taste for Optimal transport. It include more precisely:

  • Anomaly detection, ensemble methods, minimum volume set, generalized quantiles

  • Robust estimation, data depth, median-of-means

  • Optimal transport, Wasserstein distance, generative models
